AQSC Dataset Sampling and Split Documentation
=============================================

Dataset: DALTON — Indoor Air Quality Dataset with Activities of Daily Living
Source:  Karmakar et al., NeurIPS 2024 Datasets & Benchmarks Track
         https://papers.nips.cc/paper_files/paper/2024/file/81e16e41f99fe4ab9c23a0e0da8eaab2-Paper-Datasets_and_Benchmarks_Track.pdf

--- Collection Details ---
Sites:          30 indoor deployment sites
Regions:        4 geographic regions of India (North, South, East, West)
Site types:     Residential households (39.3%), studio apartments (28.6%),
                research laboratories (17.9%), food canteens (7.1%),
                classrooms (7.1%)
Duration:       6 months (summer + winter seasons)
Sampling rate:  1 Hz (continuous)
Total samples:  89.1 million (8 channels × 30 sites × ~6 months)
Occupants:      46 total; 42 actively annotating via VocalAnnot app

--- Sensor Channels ---
PM2.5 / PM10:   DUST sensor,   range 0–500 µg/m³,    error ±10 µg/m³
CO2:            MH-Z16 NDIR,   range 0–10,000 ppm,   error ±100 ppm + 6%
NO2:            MCGS,          range 0.1–10 ppm
VOC (ethanol):  MCGS,          range 1–500 ppm
CO:             MCGS,          range 5–5,000 ppm
Temperature:    DHT22,         range -40–80°C,        error ±0.5°C
Humidity:       DHT22,         range 0–100% RH,       error ±2% RH

--- Activity Labels (8 classes) ---
0: Cooking
1: Cleaning
2: Sleeping
3: Ventilation change (window open/close, fan, AC)
4: Idle (no active occupant behaviour)
5: Electronic device use
6: Eating / dining
7: Other

--- Temporal Split (no future leakage) ---
Training:       Months 1–3  (~44.6M samples per channel)
  └─ Calibration subset: First 2,000 windows of Month 1
     (used to compute σ²_max, H_max per channel and optimise θ_L, θ_H, α)
Validation:     Month 4     (~14.9M samples per channel)
Testing:        Months 5–6  (~29.6M samples per channel, WINTER season)
  └─ Deliberate domain shift: winter occupancy patterns and
     heating-driven CO2/humidity dynamics differ from summer training data

--- Calibration Convergence ---
Stage A (sensor bit-width):  converges at 500 windows
Stage B (layer rank):        converges at 2,000 windows (default)
One window = 600 seconds (10-minute DALTON HHI window)
2,000 windows ≈ 167 station-days of data

--- Hardware ---
Sensor node:    ESP32-WROOM-32 (dual-core Xtensa LX6, 240 MHz, 520 KB SRAM)
Benchmark MCU:  STM32L4R9 (Cortex-M4, 120 MHz, 640 KB SRAM, 2 MB Flash, 3.3V)
Energy sensor:  INA219 current shunt (±2.3% accuracy, 100 Hz sampling)
Connectivity:   WiFi 2.4 GHz (DALTON nodes); LoRaWAN SX1276 (STM32 benchmark)

--- Reproducibility Notes ---
All experiments use seeds {42, 43, 44, 45, 46}; results reported as mean ± std.
Statistical significance: paired t-test, p < 0.05 (Bonferroni corrected for
multiple comparisons across 30 test sites).
